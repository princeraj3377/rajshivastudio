Short-Form Video Hook

Script
Write 5 opening hooks for a 30-second vertical video about [TOPIC]. Each hook must be under 8 words, create an open loop or curiosity gap, and work as on-screen text in the first second. Avoid clickbait phrasing that the video does not deliver on.
