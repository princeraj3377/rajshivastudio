Privacy Policy

How this site handles your information — in plain language.

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This website (Rajshiva Studio) is operated by an independent video editing service. This page explains what information is collected when you visit, why it is collected, and who else can see it. If anything here is unclear, email rajshivastudio@gmail.com and ask.

Information you give us

The only place this site asks for personal information is the contact form on the home page. When you submit it, you provide:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The project details you type into the message box

This information is used for one purpose: to read your enquiry and reply to it. It is not sold, rented, or shared with advertisers, and you will not be added to any mailing list.

How form submissions are delivered

The form is processed by Web3Forms, a third-party service that forwards submissions by email. Your message passes through their systems on its way to our inbox, and is then stored in that email account. Web3Forms handles the data according to their own privacy policy.

Information collected automatically

This site does not set its own cookies and does not run analytics software. We do not track individual visitors across pages or build profiles of you.

However, some content is loaded from other companies' servers, and those companies can see your IP address and basic browser details as a normal part of delivering that content:

  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website and may log server requests.
  • Google Fonts — supplies the typefaces used across the site.
  • Cloudflare (cdnjs) — supplies the icon set.
  • YouTube — video players embedded in blog posts. These use the youtube-nocookie.com domain, which avoids setting tracking cookies unless you actually press play. Once you play a video, Google's own terms apply.

Links to other sites

This site links out to Instagram, YouTube, WhatsApp, Google Drive and various tools recommended on the resources page. Once you follow one of those links you are on someone else's website, and their privacy policy applies — not this one. We have no control over what they collect.

Downloads and resources

Files offered on the resources page may be hosted on Google Drive. Downloading them is subject to Google's terms. No account, signup or email address is required to use anything on that page.

Advertising

This site currently shows no advertising of any kind. If advertising is introduced in future — for example Google AdSense — this policy will be updated before any ads appear, and it will explain what cookies those advertisers use and how you can opt out through Google Ads Settings.

How long information is kept

Contact form emails are kept in our inbox so we can maintain a record of client conversations. If you would like your enquiry deleted, email us and it will be removed.

Your rights

You can, at any time, ask us to:

  • Tell you what information we hold about you
  • Correct anything that is wrong
  • Delete your enquiry and contact details

Send the request to rajshivastudio@gmail.com and we will action it.

Children

This site offers professional video editing services and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If how this site handles information changes, this page will be updated and the "last updated" date at the top will change. Material changes — such as adding advertising or analytics — will be reflected here before they take effect.
