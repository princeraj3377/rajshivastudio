# How to Open YouTube

## Method 1: Using a Web Browser
1. Open any web browser (Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Firefox, Safari, etc.).
2. In the address bar, type:
   ```
   https://www.youtube.com
   ```
3. Press **Enter**.
4. YouTube will open on your screen.

## Method 2: Using the YouTube App
1. Open the **YouTube** app on your mobile device.
2. Sign in to your Google account (optional).
3. Start watching videos.

## Notes
- An internet connection is required.
- You can search for videos using the search bar.
- Sign in to like, comment, subscribe, and create playlists.
