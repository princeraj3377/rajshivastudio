About Me

Turning Raw Footage Into Scroll-Stopping Stories

I'm the editor behind Rajshiva Studio, working with creators, brands and businesses that want their content to actually get watched. Over the past few years I've cut everything from fast-paced YouTube videos and viral Shorts to cinematic brand films and long-form podcasts — always with a focus on pacing, sound design and story structure.

What sets my process apart is a genuine obsession with storytelling and detail: every cut, transition and color grade is a deliberate choice, not a default. Combine that with fast turnaround times and clear communication, and the result is consistently high client satisfaction, on-time delivery, and videos that perform.
